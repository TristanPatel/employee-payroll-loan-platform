# Due diligence checklist

Verbatim 22-item checklist plus three sign-offs, taken from
`DUE_DILIGENCE_PAYROLL_CHECKLIST_v1.3.pdf`. This is the canonical spec
for the `due_diligence_checks` and `due_diligence_signoffs` tables and
the CSE review UI in Phase 5.

Every item has three possible states (`pending`, `passed`, `failed`),
plus an optional free-text note. A check can be flagged with one of three
severities: `info`, `warn`, `block` — only `passed` items with no
`block` flag let CSE submit to L1.

## Phase 1 — Initial Document Collection and Verification

| # | Item | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | Application Form | Ensure the Employee Loan Scheme Pre-Approval Application Form is filled out completely and accurately. |
| 2 | Identification | Collect a copy of the National Registration Card (NRC) and the Employee/Work ID. |
| 3 | Proof of Income | Obtain the three most recent original payslips. Verify that they are not photocopies, are free from alterations, and show consistent salary amounts. |
| 4 | Employment Contract | Collect a copy of the employment contract or confirmation letter. |
| 5 | Contact Information | Record an active phone number, especially if mobile money is the preferred payment method. |
| 6 | Bank Details | Obtain the applicant's bank account details. |

## Phase 2 — Identity and Employment Verification

| # | Item | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| 7 | NRC Verification | Verify the provided NRC against the original document. |
| 8 | Name Consistency | Ensure the employee's name matches across their ID, payslips, and employment contract. |
| 9 | Employment Confirmation | Contact the employee's supervisor or HR department to confirm the applicant's employment status. For the Due Diligence Team, a call to the CFO or Accountant is required. |
| 10 | Document Authenticity | Take copies of all original documents for the file. |

## Phase 3 — Financial Assessment and Verification

| # | Item | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| 11 | Affordability Assessment | Calculate the Debt Service Ratio (DSR) based on the applicant's net salary, existing loan deductions, and other statutory deductions. |
| 12 | Loan Terms | Verify that the requested loan amount and tenure are within the company's affordability guidelines. Adjust if necessary. |
| 13 | Bank Account Verification | Confirm that the bank account details on the pre-approval form match the payment form. |
| 14 | Settlement Calculations | Ensure all settlement and top-up calculations are correct and that the new facility meets all requirements. |

## Phase 4 — Application Processing and Approval

| # | Item | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| 15 | Signature Verification | Match all signatures on the application with the approved signature lists. |
| 16 | Signatory Confirmation | Call the signatory to confirm they have authorised and signed the pre-approval, if applicable. |
| 17 | Contract Signing | Ensure all contracts (Richmond Part A and Part B) are signed by the C.S.E/Manager and the client. |
| 18 | Loan Agreement | Have the employee sign the declaration authorising payroll deductions and the Loan Agreement Part 'B'. |

## Phase 5 — Final Documentation and Setup

| # | Item | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| 19 | Client Photo | Capture a photo of the client and upload it to their folder. This also applies to settlements / top-ups. |
| 20 | Telephone Number Confirmation | Input the client's telephone number into the Mobile Money App to confirm the name matches the client's name. |
| 21 | Mode of Payment | All clients are to be paid through a bank transfer or Mobile Money. Ensure all bank details (account number, name of bank, branch) are completed in full. |
| 22 | Document Organization | Organise all documents in a standardised order and complete this branch verification checklist. |
| 23 | Electronic Submission | Scan all documents for electronic submission to the Due Diligence Team. |

## Sign-offs (all three required before disbursement)

1. **CSE / Branch Officer** — name, signature, date.
2. **Branch Manager** — name, signature, date.
3. **Due Diligence Team** — name, signature, date.

Branch Manager cannot be the same person as the CSE (maker-checker rule
enforced in RLS).
