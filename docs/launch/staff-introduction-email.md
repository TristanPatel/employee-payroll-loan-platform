Dear colleagues,

From [go-live date] we are launching the **Richmond Finance Employee Payroll Loan Portal** — the single platform through which every employee payroll loan will be processed from application to settlement.

This is a meaningful change in how we work. Paper forms, scattered spreadsheets, and email chains for loan files are being retired. Each loan will live in one place, with a complete record of who did what and when. Your part in that workflow will be cleaner, faster, and properly auditable.

## Why we are doing this

- **Faster turnaround for borrowers.** Applications reach a Customer Service Executive within an hour of submission, not days.
- **Built-in segregation of duties.** The system enforces our maker-checker rules automatically. No one can accidentally approve their own file or sign off twice; the platform prevents it at the database level.
- **A complete audit trail.** Every action by every user — every approval, every document, every status change — is recorded and available to compliance, the auditor, and external regulators on request.
- **One source of truth.** No more "which version is current?" — there is only the portal.

## What is changing for you

- You will sign in to **portal.richmond-afri.com** with your Richmond Finance email and a temporary password. You can switch to email one-time codes after first sign-in if you prefer.
- You will land on the dashboard for your role. You will see only the data you are authorised to see, every time — no exceptions, no workarounds.
- When you cannot do something the system has blocked, it is doing its job. The handbook explains every guard so you understand why.
- Borrowers continue to be onboarded through their employer's apply link. Self-signup on the portal sign-in page is closed; only people with Richmond-issued accounts can reach the back office.

## How you will be supported

- The **Staff Handbook** is attached. Please read **at minimum the section for your role** before Day 1, and skim "About the portal" and "Maker-checker".
- The **Project Leader** is your single point of contact for anything that does not work or anything you do not understand. Their details are in the handbook.
- A **feedback log** will be open for the duration of UAT and beyond. There are no silly findings — confusion is a finding, slow screens are a finding, unclear labels are a finding. Log everything.
- Critical issues are fixed same-day. Smaller improvements go into a weekly cycle. Larger enhancements are reviewed monthly.

## What we expect from you

1. **Sign in on Day 1** and confirm you can reach your dashboard. If you cannot, tell the Project Leader immediately — that is a launch-blocker.
2. **Read your role's section** of the handbook. It is short by design.
3. **Test honestly** during UAT. If something is confusing for you, it will be confusing for a colleague. Log it.
4. **Follow the workflow as the system presents it** after go-live. Do not invent workarounds. If the system is wrong, the answer is to fix the system — not to bypass it.
5. **Protect your credentials.** Treat your portal sign-in like your bank card. If you suspect compromise, sign out everywhere from your profile screen and notify the Project Leader within the hour.

This portal carries our regulated business. The standard we hold ourselves to — discretion, accuracy, accountability — does not change. The portal helps us meet it more easily.
